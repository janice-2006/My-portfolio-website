It supports hiding not just text but also full files like .txt or .pdf without changing the image’s appearance.

MetaDocX

Metadocx is a specialized forensics tool for metadata extraction and correlation, supporting a wide range of file types including PDF, JPEG, PNG, Excel, CSV, and Word. It automates the retrieval of critical information such as modification dates, issued dates, and author attribution, providing investigators with a clear digital audit trail. The tool is designed to translate raw file data into actionable forensic intelligence by mapping the lifecycle and transmission of sensitive documents.
